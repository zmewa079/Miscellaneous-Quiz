Miscellaneous Quiz

Instructions: Choose a category to take a quiz. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible. If you can't choose, you can always click on the random quiz generator that will give you a random quiz to take. Have fun!

Scoring: Your score depends on how long you take to answer the question. Your time left over is multiplied by 50 but with each wrong attempt, your overall score decreases by 25 points.
